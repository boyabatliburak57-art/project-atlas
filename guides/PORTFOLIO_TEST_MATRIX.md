Buy, multiple buys weighted average, partial/full sell, insufficient quantity, fee/tax, dividend, cash, reversal, idempotency ve deterministic replay.

## Corporate action

Split, bonus share, rights issue, dividend, duplicate action ve fractional policy.

## Valuation/performance

## Risk

Volatility, beta, zero variance, drawdown, VaR, expected shortfall, HHI, sector concentration, insufficient history ve NaN/Infinity.

## Import/export

Valid CSV, invalid date/decimal/symbol, duplicate, atomic rollback, partial mode, formula injection, encoding, max limits ve export escaping.

## Security/E2E

Portfolio/transaction/import/export IDOR, note XSS, quota, create portfolio, buy/post, valuation, sell, performance, risk, import ve reversal.
